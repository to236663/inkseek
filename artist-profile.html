About

Hi my name is Naomi! I specialize in realistic and bold illustrative tattoos. I love collaborating with clients to bring their ideas to life, creating pieces that feel personal and timeless. With a background in both digital and traditional art, I focus on Japanese-inspired designs and anime aesthetics, blending classic Irezumi elements with contemporary illustration techniques. Each piece I create honors the rich traditions of Japanese tattooing while incorporating modern storytelling.
